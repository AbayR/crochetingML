• Pull tie to remove.
• Start crocheting!
• Ch 2 at beg of row does not
count as hdc.
• Shawl is worked from
bottom up.
Ch 102.
1st row: (RS). 1 hdc in 3rd ch from
hook. 1 hdc in each ch to end of
ch. Turn. 100 hdc.
2nd row: Ch 2. 2 hdchb in first hdc.
1 hdchb in each hdc to last hdc.
2 hdchb in last hdc. Turn. 102 sts.
Rep last row, inc 1 st at each end of
every row, until there are 200 hdc.
Fasten off at end of last row.
