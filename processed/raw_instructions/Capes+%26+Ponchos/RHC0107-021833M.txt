FINISHING
With right sides together, fold Cozy in half crosswise.
Working through both layers, join A in lower edge, sc along
edges of rows to join short ends together.
Round 1 (Wrong Side): Ch 1, turn to work opposite
foundation ch, sc in each ch around, slip st in first st to join
– 92 (104, 116) sc.
Round 2: Ch 1, *sc in next 8 (9, 10) sc, skip next sc;
repeat from * around to last 1 (3, 5) sc, sc in last 1 (3, 5)
sc, slip st in first sc – 82 (94, 106) sc.
Fasten off.
Weave in ends.
