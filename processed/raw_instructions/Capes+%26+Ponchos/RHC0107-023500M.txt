Mobius
from * across; turn – 7 shells.
Row 2: Ch 3, 2 dc in first sc, * skip next 2 dc, sc in next dc,
skip next 2 dc **, shell in next sc; repeat from * to last sc,
end at **; 3 dc in last sc; turn.
Designed by Darla Sims Row 3: Ch 1, sc in first dc, * skip next 2 dc, shell in next
sc, skip next 2 dc, sc in next dc; repeat from * across
Directions are for size Small; changes for sizes working last sc in top of ch-3; turn.
Medium, Large and Extra Large, 2X and 3X are in
parentheses. Repeat Rows 2 and 3 for pattern until 36 (40, 44, 48, 52,
56)” from beginning, end by working Row 2. Fasten off.
FInISHInG
Lay crochet strip on a flat surface. Turn beginning ch
end over to twist strip. Sew edges together while in this
position. Weave in ends.
TO WEAR
Pull over head with twist at front and adjust top to cover
shoulders as desired OR, pull down to waist with seam at
center back, then pull the front of circle up over your head.
Adjust piece to fit and fold back collar.

Aran/Gold
Knitting needles: 6.5mm [US 10½].
Yarn needle.
GAUGE: 14 sts = 4”; 26 rows = 4” in Garter stitch. CHECK
YOUR GAUGE. Use any size needles to obtain the
specified gauge.
Cast on 56 stitches.
Knit every row until piece measures 36 (40, 44, 48, 52,
56)” from beginning.
Bind off.
FInISHInG
Sew cast-on row to bind-off row, turning one end over to
sew wrong side of one end to right side of opposite end to
put a twist in length.
Weave in ends.
