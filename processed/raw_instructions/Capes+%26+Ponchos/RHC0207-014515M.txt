HOODED CROCHET BABY PONCHO | CROCHET SHOP KIT
Special Abbreviations:
Dc2tog (dc dec): [Yarn over and draw up a loop in the
next st, yarn over, pull through 2 loops] 2 times, yarn over
and draw through all loops on the hook.
PONCHO
Ch 35.
Row 1: Hdc in 3rd ch from hook and each ch across – 32
hdc.
Row 2: Ch 3 (counts as dc here and throughout), turn, dc
in same st, [dc in 7 hdc, (dc, ch 1, dc) in next hdc] 3 times,
dc in 6 hdc, 2 dc in last hdc – 36 dc.
Row 3: Ch 3, turn, dc in same st, [dc in each dc to ch-1
space, (dc, ch 1, dc) in ch-1 space] 3 times, dc in each dc
to last st, 2 dc in last st – 8 sts increased.
Repeat Row 3 until piece measures 9 (10, 11)” from
beginning.
Fasten off.
HOOD
With right side facing, join yarn at right neck edge, working
along opposite side of beginning ch, dc in 32 sts.
Row 1: Ch 3, turn, dc in each dc across.
Repeat Row 1 until Hood measures 6 (6½, 7)”.
Next Row: Ch 3, turn, dc in 11 st, [dc2tog] 4 times, dc in
12 sts.
Hooded Crochet
Fasten off.
Baby Poncho FINISHING
Fold Hood in half along last row and sew closed.
Sew 6” from lower edge along front edge of Poncho
closed.
