Row 1 (Right Side): Dc in 4th ch from hook and in each

Row 2: Ch 1, skip first dc, dc in each dc across and in top
of ch-3; turn.
Rep Row 2 until 12 (14, 16) rows in all have been worked.
Yoke: Ch 1, working in BACK LPS ONLY, sc in each dc
across and in top of ch-3; turn.
Next Row: Ch 1, sc in BACK LP ONLY of each sc across;
turn.
Rep last row 6 (7, 8) more times – 8 (9, 10) rows of sc
completed. Fasten off.
FRONT: Work same as back until 4 (5, 6) sc rows of yoke
have been completed.
Divide for Neck/Shoulder: Keeping continuity of pattern,
work over first 17 (20, 24) sc; TURN. Work 3 more rows.
Front should measure same as Back. Fasten off.
Second Shoulder: Skip center 13 sts of last long row,
attach yarn and work across remaining 17 (20, 24) sc.
Complete to correspond to first shoulder.
TURTLENECK: Ch 14 (15, 16).
Row 1 (Right Side): Sc in 2nd ch from hook and in each
ch across; turn – 13 (14, 15) sc.
Row 2: Ch 1, sc in BACK LP ONLY of each sc across;
turn.
Rows 3-40: Rep Row 2. Fasten off.
