ABBREVIATIONS
Beg = Beginning Rep = Repeat
Ch = Chain(s) Rnd = Round
Dc = Double crochet Sl st = Slip stitch
Rem = Remaining Tog = Together
MAKE THIS
INSTRUCTIONS
1st round (rnd): Ch 3 (counts as
double crochet - dc). 1 dc in each ch around.
Join with sl st to top of ch 3. 120 dc.
Double crochet
Yoh and insert hook into next ch and draw up a loop.
Yoh and draw through first 2 loops on hook.
Yoh and draw through remaining (rem) 2 loops on hook – 1 dc made.
Rep last steps in each ch around - 120 dc made.
2nd rnd: Ch 3. 1 dc in each dc around. Join with sl st to top of ch 3.
Rep last rnd 10 times more. Fasten off at end of last rnd.
To fasten off the yarn, cut yarn leaving a long tail. Pull end through
loop on hook and pull gently to tighten.
Weave in all yarn tails.
FINISHING
Tassels (Make 6)
Holding 1 strand each of A and B tog, wrap yarn around
a 3" [7.5 cm] piece of cardboard 10 times. Tie securely
at one end. Cut across other end. Wrap yarn 3 times
around tassel 1" [2.5 cm] from tied end. Fasten securely.
Trim tassels to approx 2" [5 cm] length.
Attach Tassels to Snood, having 3 on either side edge, evenly
spaced.
