Special Stitches
V-st (V-stitch) = (Dc, ch 1, dc) in indicated stitch.
lg-V-st (Large V-stitch) = (Dc, ch 2, dc) in indicated stitch.
shell = (2 dc, ch 1, 2 dc) in indicated stitch.
picot = Ch 2, slip st in last stitch made.
3-dc Cl (3 double crochet cluster) = Yarn over, insert
hook in indicated stitch and draw up a loop, yarn over and
draw through 2 loops on hook (2 loops remain on hook),
[yarn over, insert hook in same stitch and draw up a loop,
yarn over and draw through 2 loops on hook] 2 times, yarn
over and draw through all 4 loops on hook.
5-dc Cl (5 double crochet cluster) = Yarn over, insert
hook in indicated stitch and draw up a loop, yarn over and
draw through 2 loops on hook (2 loops remain on hook),
[yarn over, insert hook in same stitch and draw up a loop,
yarn over and draw through 2 loops on hook] 4 times, yarn
over and draw through all 6 loops on hook.

Small Flower (Make 2)
With smaller hook and leaving long beginning tail for
sewing, ch 6; join with slip st in first ch to form a ring.
Round 1: Ch 3 (counts as dc here and throughout), 2 dc in
ring, ch 2, [3 dc in ring, ch 2] 5 times; join with slip st in top
of beginning ch—Six 3-dc groups and 6 ch-2 spaces.
Round 2: Slip st in next dc (center dc of first 3-dc group),
*ch 2, 5-dc Cl in next ch-2 space, picot, ch 2, slip st in
center dc of next 3-dc group; repeat from * around; join
with slip st in first slip st—6 clusters.
Fasten off.
Large Flower
With larger hook, ch 6; join with slip st in first ch to from a ring.
Round 1: Work same as Round 1 of small flower—Six
3-dc groups and 6 ch-2 spaces.
Round 2: Slip st in next dc (center dc of first 3-dc group),
*ch 2, 3-dc Cl in next ch-2 space, picot, ch 2, slip st in
center dc of next 3-dc group; repeat from * around; join
with slip st in first slip st—6 clusters.
Fasten off.

FINISHING
Weave in all ends. Sew button to top left corner of
back yoke. Use spaces between stitches on overlap for
buttonhole. Sew small flower to front of yoke, near lower
edge of yoke and to the right of center.
Place remaining small flower on top of larger flower.
Thread beginning tail of small flower onto yarn needle,
sewing through all three layers (small flower, large flower
and headband), sew flowers to headband.
Mist lightly and block, if desired.
