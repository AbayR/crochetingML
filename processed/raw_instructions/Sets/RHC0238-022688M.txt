SPECIAL STITCHES
FPdc (front post double crochet) = Yo, insert hook from
front to back to front again, around post of indicated st, yo
and draw up a loop, [yo and draw through 2 loops on hook]
twice. Skip the stitch directly behind the FPdc before making
the next stitch.
NOTE
To change color in a slip stitch, insert hook in indicated
stitch, drop working color and pick up new color, draw new
color through loops on hook. Fasten off old color.
