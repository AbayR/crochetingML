HAT

Row 3: Join B with slip st between first 2 sts of last row,
ch 3, 2 dc in same space, sc in 3rd dc of next shell, *shell
in next sc, sc in 3rd dc of next shell, repeat from * across,
3 dc in last sc. Fasten off.
Row 4: Hold piece with right side facing, join C with slip st
in 3rd ch of beginning ch-3 on last row worked, ch 1, sc in
same ch, shell in next sc, *sc in 3rd dc of next shell, shell
in next sc, repeat from * across, sc in last dc. Fasten off.
Rows 4-14: Repeat Rows 3 and 4, in following color
sequence: 1 row A, 1 row B, 1 row C.
Rows 15: Repeat Row 3.
ASSEMBLY
With A, whipstitch ends of rows together on wrong side to
form back seam.

BAND
Round 1: Join A with slip st in sc at back seam; ch 3, dc in
each sc around, join with slip st in 3rd ch of beginning ch-3. ABBREVIATIONS: A, B, C = color A, B, C; ch = chain;
Fasten off. dc = double crochet; hdc = half double crochet;
Round 2: Join C with slip st in dc at back seam; ch 3, dc in mm = millimeters; sc = single crochet; st(s) = stitch(es);
each sc around, join with slip st in 3rd ch of beginning ch-3. yo = yarn over needle; * or ** = repeat whatever follows
Fasten off. the * or ** as indicated.
Round 3: Join B with slip st in dc at back seam; ch 3, dc in
each sc around, join with slip st in 3rd ch of beginning ch-3.
Fasten off.
Round 4: Join A with slip st in dc at back seam; ch 1, * sc
in 1st and 2nd dc, 2 sc in 3 dc, repeat from * around; join
with slip st in beginning ch-1. Fasten off.
FINISHING
Weave in ends. Weave 12-inch strand of B through sts on
Row 15. Gather to close top opening and secure ends.
SCARF
With A, ch 257.
Row 1 (Right Side): Shell in 5th ch from hook, *sk next
3 chs, sc in next ch, sk next 3 chs, shell in next ch, repeat
from * across to last 4 chs, sk next 3 chs, sc in last ch.
Fasten off.
Row 2: Hold piece with right side facing, join B with slip st
in space formed by beginning 4 sk chs on last row worked,
ch 3, 2 dc in same space, sc in 3rd dc of next shell, *shell
in next sc, sc in 3rd dc of next shell, repeat from * across
to last sc, 3 dc in last sc. Fasten off.
Row 3: Hold piece with right side facing, join C with slip st
in 3rd ch of beginning ch-3 on last row worked, ch 1, sc in
same ch, shell in next sc, *sc in 3rd dc of next shell, shell
in next sc, repeat from * across to last 3 dc, sk next 2 dc,
sc in last dc. Fasten off.
Rows 4-11: Repeat Rows 2 and 3, in following color
sequence: 1 row A, 1 row B, 1 row C.
Row 12: With C, repeat Row 2.
FINISHING
Weave in ends.
FRINGE
Cut 8-inch lengths of each color. Fold 2 strands of A in
half. Draw folded end through side of Row 1; pull ends
through fold and tighten knot. Matching color of knot with
color of row and using 2 strands for each knot, tie knot in
side of each row on each short end of scarf. Trim ends
even.
