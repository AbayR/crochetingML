Edging
Round 1: Working in ends of rows down
first side, 2 sc in each row; working across
opposite side of foundation ch, sc in each ch
across; working in ends of rows up other side,
2 sc in each row, sc in each st across; join
with slip st in first st. Fasten off.
FINISHING
On right side of diaper cover, place large
buttons on Row 3 or 4 approximately 3
stitches from outer edge. With a strand of A,
sew in place. Using spaces between stitches
as buttonholes, fold ends of last rows over first
rows and button in place.
Optional: Apply fabric sealant to knots
securing buttons to reduce the risk of
detachment.
Weave in ends.
ABBREVIATIONS
A, B, C = Color A, Color B, Color C, etc.; ch =
chain; dc = double crochet; hdc = half double
crochet; sc = single crochet; st(s) = stitch(es);
[ ] = work directions in brackets the number
of times specified; * = repeat whatever
follows the * as indicated.
