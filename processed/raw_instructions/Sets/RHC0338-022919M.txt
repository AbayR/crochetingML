Join Halves: Hold short edges of both halves together;
working through both thicknesses across short edge, slip
st in corner ch-1 space, slip st in each space between 2-dc
groups across to next corner, slip st in corner ch-1 space.
Fasten off. If preferred, fasten off and sew short edges
together.
Corner Petals
With Right Side facing and larger hook, join matching color
with sc in corner ch-1 space of scarf, [ch 6, sc in same
ch-1 space] 5 times. Fasten off. Repeat to work petals in
remaining 3 corners.
FInISHInG
Weave in ends.
HEADBAnD
With larger hook and 2 strands held together, ch 14.
Row 1: Dc in 4th ch from hook (beginning ch counts as
first dc), [ch 1, skip next ch, dc in next ch] 5 times, turn—7
dc and 5 ch-1 spaces.
Row 2: Ch 3 (counts as first dc here and throughout), [dc
in next ch-1 space, ch 1, skip next dc] 5 times, dc in top of
turning ch, turn.
Repeat Row 2 until piece measures about 20” from begin-
ning, slightly stretched.
Join Ends: Hold foundation ch and last row together, with
sts matching, working through both thicknesses, slip st in
each st across. Fasten off.
Ruffle
Join 1 strand with sc in end of any row, ch 5, sc in end of
same row, *ch 5, (sc, ch 5, sc) in end of next row; repeat
from * around, ch 5; join with slip st in first sc. Fasten off.
With 1 strand repeat around other edge.
FInISHInG
Weave in ends.
