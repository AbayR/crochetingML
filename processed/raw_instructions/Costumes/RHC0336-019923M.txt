Lightsaber
Prepare wrapping paper tube for insertion
by applying adhesive tape across one end,
stuffing tube firmly with fiberfill, and taping
other end closed. For added stability, wrap the
tube with tape. Insert tube into lightsaber. The
fit should be snug. Close bottom by wrapping
loop around bottom button.
Weave in all ends.
