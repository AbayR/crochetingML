INSTRUCTIONS
SLEEVES (worked in the round)
1st rnd: (RS). Join B with sl st at side seam. Ch 1. Work 36 (38-41-
41-43) sc evenly up to shoulder seam. Work 36 (38-41-41-43) sc
evenly down to side seam. Join with sl st to first sc. 72 (76-82-82-
86) sc.
2nd and 3rd rnds: Ch 3. 1 dc in each st around. Join with sl st to
top of ch 3.
Fasten off.
Bottom Edging
1st rnd: (RS). Join B with sl st in left side seam at bottom edge.
Ch 1. Work 1 sc in each dc and 1 sc in each ch-2 sp across lower
edge of Back and Front pieces. Join with sl st to first sc.
2nd and 3rd rnds: Ch 3. 1 dc in each st around. Join with sl st to
top of ch 3.
Fasten off.
