Button Band and Lower Edging
Row 1: With right side facing and smaller hook, attach
yarn at left front neck edge; ch 1, work sc evenly down
edge, 3 sc in corner, work sc across lower edge, 3 sc in
corner, and work sc evenly up right front edge; turn.
Row 2: Ch 1, sc in each sc around, working 3 sc in each
corner as before; turn.
Buttonhole Row 3 (Work on right front for girls, left
front for boys): Mark positions for 4 buttonholes on
appropriate front. Working corners as before, ch 1, [sc in
each sc to marker, ch 1, skip 1 sc] 4 times, sc in each sc to
end. Fasten off. Weave in ends.
Sew on buttons to correspond to buttonholes.
