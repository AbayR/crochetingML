GAUGE: 11 dc = 4”; 4 rows = 4”. CHECK YOUR GAUGE. Use
any size hook to obtain the gauge.
NOTE: Fringe can be added to any heavyweight skirt.
SKIRT
Base Stitches
With right side facing and 1 strand of A, sew a straight line
of running stitches along lower edge of skirt with each stitch
measuring ½” long. Knot ends securely.
With B, sew a second line of running stitches ½” above the
previous line. Knot ends securely.
With A, sew a third line of running stitches ½” above the previous
line. Knot ends securely.
Lower Fringe
Round 1: Join A in 1 base stitch at center of back of skirt, ch 3
(counts as dc here and throughout), 2 dc in same space, 3 dc in
each base stitch around, slip st in top of beginning ch to join.
Round 2: Ch 2 (counts as hdc here and throughout), hdc in each
dc around, slip st in top of beginning ch.
Round 3: [Ch 20, slip st in next hdc] around.
Fasten off.
Center Fringe
Round 1: Join B in 1 base stitch at center of back of skirt, ch 3
(counts as dc here and throughout), 2 dc in same space, 3 dc in
each base stitch around, slip st in top of beginning ch to join.
Round 2: Ch 2 (counts as hdc here and throughout), hdc in each
dc around, slip st in top of beginning ch.
Round 3: [Ch 20, slip st in next hdc] around.
Fasten off.
Top Fringe
Diva Denim Remix Skirt
Round 1: Join A in 1 base stitch at center of back of skirt, ch 2
(counts as hdc here and throughout), 2 hdc in same space, 3 hdc
in each base stitch around, slip st in top of beginning ch to join.
Transform a skirt from plain to lively and fun with
Round 2: [Ch 15, slip st in next hdc] around.
rows of crocheted ruffles. We show it in denim, Fasten off.
but other fabric skirts work just as well.
FINISHING
Weave in ends.
