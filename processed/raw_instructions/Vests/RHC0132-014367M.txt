NOTES: Vest is made in two pieces. One side is crocheted
lengthwise; the second side is crocheted widthwise. If
desired, Vest can be made working both sides the same.
SIDE 1
With larger hook, ch 27 (30, 34, 37, 40).
Row 1: Sc in 2nd ch from hook and in each ch across – 26
(29, 33, 36, 39) sc.
Row 2: Ch 1, sc in each sc – 26 (29, 33, 36, 39) sc.
Repeat Row 2 until piece measures 30 (31, 31, 32, 32)”
from beginning.
Fasten off.
SIDE 2
With larger hook, ch 100 (103, 103, 106, 106) sts.
Row 1: Sc in 2nd ch from hook and in each ch across – 99
(102, 102, 105, 105) sc.
Row 2: Ch 1, sc in each sc – 99 (102, 102, 105, 105) sc.
Repeat Row 2 until piece measures 8 (9, 10, 11, 12)” from
beginning.
Fasten off.
