FINISHING: Sew shoulder seams.
Front “cabled” seam: With right side of fronts facing you,
beginning at lower edge, hold the first ch-7 loops from the
right and left sides together with one hand. * Pick up the
next loop from each side with free hand; pull the 2nd set
of loops through the first set of loops; repeat from * to form
center seam until approximately 16 (16, 17, 17, 18)” from
beginning or desired length.
Neckband: Separate the last 2 loops and use one for
looping up the left front neck edge and use the other one
for looping up the right front neck edge until they meet at
the center back of the neck. With yarn needle and small
amount of yarn, sew the remaining free loops to the wrong
side to secure.
